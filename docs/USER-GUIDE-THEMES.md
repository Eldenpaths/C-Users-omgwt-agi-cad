## What are Themes and Why Use Them?

Themes in AGI-CAD are pre-designed visual styles that change the look and feel of your workspace. Think of them as different outfits for your application! They modify colors, fonts, and even animations to create a unique atmosphere.

**Why use themes?**
*   **Personalization:** Make AGI-CAD feel more like *your* space.
*   **Comfort:** Choose a theme that's easier on your eyes for long sessions.
*   **Focus:** Some themes are designed to reduce distraction, while others enhance specific types of content.
*   **Aesthetics:** Simply enjoy a beautiful and engaging interface!

---

## How to Switch Themes

Switching themes in AGI-CAD is quick and easy!

### üñ±Ô∏è Using the Palette Icon

1.  Look for the **Palette icon** (looks like an artist's palette or a color wheel) usually located in the top-right corner of the application.
2.  Click on the **Palette icon**. A dropdown menu will appear showing all available themes.
3.  Click on the name of the theme you wish to activate. The application will instantly switch to your chosen theme!

### ‚å®Ô∏è Using a Keyboard Shortcut

For an even faster way to cycle through themes, you can use a keyboard shortcut:

*   Press `Ctrl + Shift + T` (or `Cmd + Shift + T` on Mac).
*   Each time you press this combination, AGI-CAD will cycle to the next available theme. Keep pressing until you find the one you like!

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Will my theme choice be saved?**
A: Yes! AGI-CAD remembers your last chosen theme. It will automatically load your preferred theme the next time you open the application.

**Q: Can I create my own themes?**
A: Currently, AGI-CAD provides a curated set of themes. Custom theme creation is not yet available to end-users, but we are always exploring new features!

**Q: Do themes affect performance?**
A: Our themes are optimized for performance. While some themes include subtle animations, they are designed to run smoothly on most modern systems. If you experience any issues, please refer to the Troubleshooting Guide.
